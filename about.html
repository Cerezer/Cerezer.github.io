About Me

Since high school, I've known I wanted to be a biologist. My passion for ecology and the complex mechanisms of nature was ignited during my bachelor’s studies.

Originally from Ivorá, a charming countryside town nestled between the Atlantic Forest and the Pampa grasslands in southern Brazil, my roots have deeply influenced my love for the natural world. I pursued my undergraduate and graduate studies in Santa Maria, Brazil, where my academic journey began.

“Understanding biodiversity patterns requires a deep dive into nature’s evolutionary history.”

When I'm not immersed in research, I enjoy being outdoors—playing football, volleyball, paddleboarding, or hiking. These activities keep me active and grounded in the beauty of nature.

About Me

Hello! I'm Felipe O. Cerezer, a biologist passionate about understanding large-scale patterns of biodiversity. I am currently a postdoctoral fellow at the Czech Academy of Sciences and Charles University in the Czech Republic. My research lies at the intersection of macroecology, macroevolution, and biogeography. I aim to uncover how historical, evolutionary, and ecological processes shape the distribution of life on Earth.

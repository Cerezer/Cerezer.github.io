Welcome

I'm Felipe Cerezer, a macroecologist and evolutionary biologist passionate about uncovering the processes that shape biodiversity through time and across space. My research bridges macroecology, macroevolution, and biogeography, using phylogenies, spatial data, and species traits to explore how historical, ecological, and evolutionary dynamics drive large-scale patterns of species richness. I am currently a postdoctoral fellow at Charles University in Prague.

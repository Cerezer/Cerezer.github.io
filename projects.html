Research Projects

Explore my research projects related to macroecology, data science, and biogeography. I apply cutting-edge statistical and machine learning techniques to address ecological questions and enhance our understanding of biodiversity patterns.

Spatial Analysis for Retail Site Selection

This project applies spatial analysis methods to identify optimal locations for new retail stores. By integrating geospatial data and predictive modeling, the project aims to reduce business risks and improve site selection accuracy.

Species Richness in Terrestrial Tetrapods

Through path analysis and macroecological data, this project examines the factors influencing species richness in terrestrial tetrapod clades. It investigates how ecological, evolutionary, and environmental factors shape diversity patterns across large geographical scales.
